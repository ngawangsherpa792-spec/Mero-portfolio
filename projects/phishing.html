Phishing Simulation

Comprehensive simulation platform to test and educate users on sophisticated attacks, identifying human vulnerabilities in the security chain.

Social EngineeringHTML/JSAnalytics

Project Overview

Phishing remains the #1 entry point for cyberattacks. This project was developed as a controlled training tool for organizations to measure their staff's susceptibility to social engineering.

Key Features

  • High-fidelity templates for popular services.
  • Automated campaign management and email tracking.
  • Non-intrusive data collection (no credentials stored, only click-through rates).
  • Detailed analytics and vulnerability reporting.

The Solution

By simulating real-world attacks, organizations can pinpoint departments or individuals requiring additional security awareness training, significantly reducing the actual risk of a breach.
